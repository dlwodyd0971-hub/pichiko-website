Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025  ·  Last Updated: April 2025
PEACHKO ("we", "our", "us") operates a K-Beauty mobile makeup and hair service in Seoul, Korea. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website and services.

01 Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use our services:

  • Account information — email address and password (managed securely via Firebase Authentication)
  • Booking details — name, hotel/address in Seoul, selected date and time, service type, preferred artist, and any special requests
  • Usage data — pages visited, language preference, and interaction logs collected automatically
  • Device information — browser type, operating system, and IP address for security and analytics purposes

02 How We Use Your Information

  • To process and confirm your beauty service bookings
  • To match you with the right artist and coordinate your appointment
  • To send booking confirmation and service-related communications
  • To prevent duplicate bookings and protect against fraud
  • To improve our website, services, and customer experience
  • To comply with applicable laws and regulations

03 Firebase & Third-Party Services

Our website uses Google Firebase (Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore) to manage user accounts and store booking data. Firebase is operated by Google LLC and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

  • Booking data is stored in Firebase Firestore (servers located in the US or Asia)
  • Passwords are never stored by PEACHKO — they are managed entirely by Firebase Authentication
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any advertising networks
Firebase Privacy Policy: firebase.google.com/support/privacy

04 Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. Booking records are kept for a minimum of 1 year for operational and legal purposes. You may request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 06).

05 Cookies & Analytics

Our website may use session cookies to maintain your login state and language preferences. We do not use third-party tracking cookies or advertising cookies. No personal data is shared with advertising networks.

06 Your Rights

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Objection — object to processing of your personal data in certain circumstances
  • Portability — request a machine-readable copy of your data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the address below.

07 Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 14. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

08 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
